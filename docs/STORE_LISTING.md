### English

**n8n Trigger: Your AI & Automation Sidekick**

Bridge the gap between your web browsing, Large Language Models (LLMs), and n8n automation workflows. n8n Trigger adds a non-intrusive, powerful sidebar to your browser, allowing you to interact with AI and trigger complex automations without leaving your current page.

**ğŸš€ Key Features:**

*   **Universal Sidebar Interface**: Access a collapsible chat interface on any webpage.
*   **Multi-LLM Support**: Bring your own keys! Supports OpenAI, OpenRouter, and GLM (ZhipuAI). Switch between models instantly.
*   **Seamless n8n Integration**: Trigger your n8n workflows directly from the browser. Send selected text, user input, and full page context to your webhooks.
*   **Context-Aware AI**:
    *   **Selection Mode**: Highlight text to summarize, translate, or explain it instantly.
    *   **Page Context**: Feed the entire webpage content to AI or n8n for comprehensive analysis.
*   **Privacy First**: Your API keys and configuration are stored **locally** in your browser (`chrome.storage.local`). We adhere to a "Zero Cloud" policy for your sensitive credentialsâ€”they never leave your device except to reach the providers you configure.

**ğŸ’¡ Use Cases:**

*   **Data Extraction**: Send webpage content to an n8n workflow to extract specific data into Notion, Google Sheets, or Slack.
*   **Quick Automation**: Trigger everyday tasks like "Save to Read Later" or "Create Jira Ticket" directly from the context menu.
